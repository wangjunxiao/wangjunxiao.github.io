About Me

I currently hold the position of Postdoctoral Fellow at the KAUST Privacy-Awareness, Responsibility and Trustworthy (PART) Lab, working with Prof. Di Wang.

In 2021-2023, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at the PolyU Edge Intelligence Laboratory (PEIL) of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, working with Prof. Song Guo. In 2018-2019, I was a Visiting Student at the Networks Research Group of Queen Mary University of London, working with Prof. Steve Uhlig. I completed my PhD at Dalian University of Technology in 2020, where my mentor was Prof. Keqiu Li. Prior to that, I earned my MEng and BE degrees in 2017, 2014.

Research Interests

I'm broadly interested in machine learning and systems with a special focus on federated learning, trustworthy machine learning and networking.
